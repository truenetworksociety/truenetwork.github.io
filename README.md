# TrueNetwork Community Solutions Society

**Community intelligence, collaborative infrastructure, and place-based innovation for public benefit.**

TrueNetwork Community Solutions Society is a nonprofit organization based in British Columbia, Canada. The Society advances community-driven research, digital infrastructure, and collaborative innovation to strengthen resilience, equity, and sustainable development.

Our work connects community knowledge, participatory research, digital systems, and collaborative action so organizations, researchers, governments, municipalities, and community partners can better understand local needs, share insights, and create stronger outcomes together.

---

## About the Society

TrueNetwork Community Solutions Society operates on a nonprofit basis for public benefit. The Society focuses on three connected areas of work:

* **Applied Research & Community Knowledge**
  Supporting evidence-informed planning, participatory evaluation, program co-creation, and asset-based community development.

* **Digital Infrastructure for Social Impact**
  Developing and operating digital tools, data systems, and collaborative platforms that make community knowledge more accessible, usable, and actionable.

* **Programs & Community Innovation**
  Designing and supporting initiatives that improve collective well-being, resilience, sustainability, inclusion, and place-based innovation.

The Society works with nonprofit organizations, community partners, municipalities, governments, researchers, and policymakers to support more connected, equitable, and sustainable communities.

## TrueNetwork Platform

The Society also supports a product called **TrueNetwork Platform**.

TrueNetwork Platform helps nonprofits, researchers, students, and funders discover and reuse community knowledge. It is designed to make collaboration easier, reduce duplication of effort, and strengthen collective action across sectors.

### What the platform does

* Helps users **search community projects and knowledge**
* Supports **mapping initiatives to the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)**
* Enables users to **find collaborators and funders**
* Encourages organizations to **reuse and share insights** for greater reach and efficiency

### Platform purpose

TrueNetwork Platform is built to help communities and organizations:

* Discover what others are already doing
* Strengthen collaboration across sectors
* Share knowledge more effectively
* Accelerate community impact

Learn more: [truenetworksociety.ca/platform](https://truenetworksociety.ca/platform)

## Mission and Public Benefit

TrueNetwork Community Solutions Society exists to support community well-being through research, education, digital infrastructure, and collaborative action. Any resources, revenues, or surpluses are used solely to advance the Society's nonprofit purposes.

Its work may include initiatives related to:

* Community well-being and health equity
* Climate resilience and environmental sustainability
* Housing and homelessness
* Mobility and active transportation
* Recreation, learning, and community activation
* Sustainable regeneration and place-based innovation
